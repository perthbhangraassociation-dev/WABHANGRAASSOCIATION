Who we are

The official home of Punjabi folk arts in Western Australia.

We create opportunities for people of all ages, cultures and backgrounds to participate in cultural activities that build confidence, strengthen social cohesion and celebrate diversity.

Our vision is to establish Western Australia as a recognised centre for Punjabi folk arts while ensuring that traditions are preserved, shared and celebrated across generations.

Through professionally organised programs, competitions, performances, workshops and educational initiatives, we inspire participation, develop young leaders and promote healthy lifestyles through dance.

Our purpose
“Preserve the tradition. Empower the next generation. Share Punjab with the world.”

What we do

Built around culture, movement and belonging.

Our activities support cultural education, physical wellbeing, teamwork, discipline, leadership and multicultural understanding.

Our flagship event

Perth City
Bhangra Cup

The Perth City Bhangra Cup is Western Australia's first interstate Bhangra competition, bringing together teams, judges, artists and volunteers from across Australia and overseas.

History was created

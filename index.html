Who we are

THE OFFICIAL
HOME OF PUNJABI
FOLK ARTS IN
WESTERN
AUSTRALIA.

We create opportunities for people of all ages, cultures and backgrounds to participate in cultural activities that build confidence, strengthen social cohesion and celebrate diversity.

Our vision is to establish Western Australia as a recognised centre for Punjabi folk arts while ensuring that traditions are preserved, shared and celebrated across generations.

Through professionally organised programs, competitions, performances, workshops and educational initiatives, we inspire participation, develop young leaders and promote healthy lifestyles through dance.
